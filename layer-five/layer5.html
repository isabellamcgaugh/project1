01. The Desire

The desire that even as you live your life, you are secretly wishing it would flow backward, so you could see the beginning of the story before the end.

It is the curiosity for the seeds of things, rather than the final harvest.

02. Fragment I

"Let the sand fall upward," the poet pleads. We want to see the face of the stranger before they became the love of our life. We want to hear the silence of the room before the first word was spoken.

Avenoir is the hunger for the 'once upon a time' while already living the 'happily ever after.'

03. Fragment II

"The movie plays in reverse.
The smoke becomes the fire.
The old man becomes the child.
Everything is new again."
