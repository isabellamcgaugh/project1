ENTRY NO. 01

Hiraeth

/hiraɪθ/

Definition

A homesickness for a home to which you cannot return, a home which maybe never was; the nostalgia and the grief and the regret for the lost places of your past.

Origin: Welsh

Translation

LANGUAGE: SOUND

We translate this into a low buzzing static, as distant music cuts in and out. It is the sound of a radio tuned to a station that no longer broadcasts.
