Definition

The restless, phantom sensation of being late for an appointment that doesn't exist; the uneasy feeling that you are supposed to be somewhere else, doing something more important, but you have forgotten what it is or where you are supposed to be.

Origin: From Middle English ficchen (to hasten or fidget) + aching.
